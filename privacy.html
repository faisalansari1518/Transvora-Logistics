Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This privacy notice explains how Transvora Logistics handles information submitted through this website.

Information we collect

We may collect your name, company name, phone number, email address, pickup and delivery locations, cargo details and any information you provide in an enquiry.

How we use information

  • To respond to quotation and service enquiries.
  • To plan transportation or warehousing services.
  • To communicate about an active or proposed shipment.
  • To maintain business and compliance records where required.

WhatsApp and external services

When you choose to send an enquiry through WhatsApp, your message is processed according to WhatsApp’s terms and privacy practices. Map and social-media links may also open third-party services.

Data sharing

Information may be shared with authorised employees, service providers or transport partners only where necessary to evaluate or fulfil your requirement, or where required by law.

Security and retention

Reasonable steps are taken to protect business information. Data is retained only for as long as needed for enquiries, operations, accounting, compliance or dispute resolution.

Your choices

You may ask us to correct or delete information that is no longer required, subject to legal and operational recordkeeping obligations.

Contact

For privacy questions, email info@transvoralogistics.in.
